About Me

Hi, I'm Chintan — a Shopify Full Stack Developer with 4+ years of experience building custom, high-performance eCommerce stores.

Over the years, I've helped brands across fashion, beauty, jewelry, food, supplements, and lifestyle industries turn designs into scalable Shopify experiences.

From pixel-perfect Figma-to-Shopify builds to complex theme customization, custom functionality, and performance optimization, I focus on creating stores that not only look great but also drive measurable business results.
Every store I build is designed to balance performance, scalability, and conversion.
Today, I help brands:
  • Launch custom Shopify stores from Figma designs
  • Build scalable themes using Liquid, metafields & metaobjects
  • Improve store speed, UX, and mobile performance
  • Implement custom features beyond standard Shopify limitations
  • Optimize storefronts for higher conversion rates
  • Scale and maintain growing eCommerce businesses
I don't just build Shopify stores — I build stores that grow businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer as a Shopify developer?

I offer full Shopify store development, redesign, theme customization, speed optimization, app integrations, checkout enhancements, and technical setup. I also help with SEO

Do you build complete e-commerce websites from scratch?

Yes. I design and develop complete e-commerce websites with clean UI, fast performance, responsive layouts, and a fully optimized user journey — from homepage to checkout.

How much does a Shopify store development project cost?

Pricing depends on the store’s complexity, number of pages, custom features, and integrations. Basic stores start affordable, and advanced builds include custom design, automation, and funnels. Contact me for a clear quote.

Can you redesign an existing Shopify website?

Absolutely. I redesign outdated Shopify stores to improve layout, speed, conversions, and user experience. This includes UI cleanup, product restructuring, navigation fixes, trust elements, and mobile optimization.

Do you provide e-commerce consultation for new brands?

Yes. I guide brands with end-to-end e-commerce strategy — covering development, UX, SEO, analytics, inventory flow, and delivery planning.

Do you offer monthly support or maintenance?

Yes. I provide monthly support plans for store updates, optimization, product uploads, bug fixes, and SEO improvements.

How long does it take to build a Shopify store?

A standard Shopify store takes 5–10 days, while custom or advanced stores may take 2–4 weeks depending on features and scope.

Will you handle everything from development to delivery?

Yes. I support brands through the full journey — development, design, optimization, SEO, and delivery — making it easy for businesses to launch and scale without juggling multiple freelancers.
